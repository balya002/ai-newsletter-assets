📊 Industry Insight: What Deloitte Is Seeing in Enterprise AI (2026)
Deloitte’s 2026 State of AI in the Enterprise shows organizations crossing a critical threshold: AI is no longer experimental, but most companies are still extracting incremental value rather than transformational impact.
  • Production is the new bottleneck: access to AI tools has expanded quickly, but scaling them into core workflows remains uneven.
  • Productivity leads, reinvention lags: efficiency gains are common, while true process and business-model redesign is still rare.
  • Agentic AI is accelerating: autonomous systems are moving faster than governance, creating both opportunity and risk.
🤖 AI is becoming part of everyday tools (not a separate destination)
Copilot lives inside Outlook, Teams, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint — which means less copy/paste and less context-switching.
  • Faster replies + recaps directly from Outlook
  • Easier doc/deck edits without jumping tools
  • More time for higher-value work (less admin)

Ready-to-copy prompts you can use immediately.
📌 Template #1: “Weekly Account Snapshot”

Use case: Monday planning, pre-meeting prep, account coverage

Give me a concise account snapshot for [Client Name] based on my Outlook emails, Teams chats, and meetings from the last 30 days.

Include:
• Key updates
• Open action items
• Risks or blockers
📌 Template #2: “Meeting Summary & Follow-Ups”

Use case: Post-meeting recaps, follow-ups, documentation

Summarize this meeting with:
• Key decisions
• Action items with owners
• Follow-ups needed

Tip: This works best when meeting transcription is enabled.
